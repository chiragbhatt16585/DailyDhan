Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your transactions and categories are stored locally on your device using a secure database. You stay in control.

Do you charge any fees?

No. The app is free for users. We may earn a small affiliate commission when you shop via links shown inside the app.

Does it work offline?

Yes. You can add and view transactions offline. Some features like ads may require internet, but your core data does not.

Which currencies are supported?

DailyDhan supports world currencies (₹, $, €, and more) so you can use it no matter where you live or travel.
